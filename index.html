🚗 Step 1 — Parking

Step 01
Where to park
Park alongside the property wall on the street. You'll see the orange and green wall - that's us! Leave enough room for traffic to pass comfortably.

🚪 Step 2 — The Gate

Step 02
Opening the main gate
Slide your hand through the bars and feel for the black latch mechanism.
Step 02a
Reaching through
Reach through the gate and find the latch on the inside, in the middle. Lift to release — the gate will swing open.
💡 TipThe latch is right at hand height in the centre of the gate frame. Feel for it and it'll pop right open!
Step 02b
The latch — close up
The green latch is the large curved handle in the middle of the gate. Push it down or to the side to release. Easy!

🔑 Step 3 — Your Door

Step 03
Unlocking your room
Head up the stairs to your door. Your code is the last 4 digits of your phone number. Enter them on the smart lock keypad and the deadbolt will release automatically.
🔐 Last 4 digits of your number
📱 Can't remember?Check your booking confirmation — your code is also listed there. Or message us any time!

🔒 Locking the door

To lock the smart lock, press the button between the 0 and the 9 on the keypad. The deadbolt will click into place automatically — no code needed.

The lock button
